Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Mimoovie ("we", "our", "the app") is a streaming discovery app that helps you find movies and series available on your streaming services. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data we collect

  • Email address — only if you create an account (via Supabase Auth). Used solely for login and account recovery.
  • Watched movies & watchlist — stored locally in your browser (localStorage). If you sign in, synced to our database so you can access your list across devices.
  • Settings — your selected streaming services and region, stored locally and optionally synced to your account.

Data we do NOT collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers

Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication and cloud sync. Data stored in EU. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — movie metadata, posters, ratings. TMDB Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions. Standard server logs (IP, request path) retained briefly. Vercel Privacy Policy

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Local data (localStorage) can be cleared through your browser settings.

Children

Mimoovie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions? Contact us at privacy@mimoovie.app
